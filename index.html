The Impact Of Porches On The Environment

The materials that are used to build porches, the energy required to heat and cool them, and the possibility of lead exposure—especially in older homes where paint may be peeling on porch surfaces—can all have an adverse effect on the environment. However, when porches are designed with sustainability in mind, they can also benefit the environment by letting in natural light and ventilation and possibly lowering the amount of energy used in the house.
